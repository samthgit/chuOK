Guide 2: Logging in

There are two ways to log in to chuOK:

1. Nav-Bar on Main Page:
Press the navigation bar on the main page to access the navigation menu. Then click on the icon at the bottom to access the Log In page.

2. Log In Button on Sign Up Page:
Pressing the LogIn link on the login page will get you to the Log In page.

Once you are on the Log In page, fill in the required fields:

1. Email: Enter a valid email address that you have access to.

2. Password: Create a strong password that you will remember.

Then you will ready to go. Welcome back!
