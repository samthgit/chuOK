Guide 3: Library

How to access the library:

Sign In. In the Home Page click the "Library" button.

Once in, you have two options: Articles and Phrases.

1. Articles Section:
When clicking on the Articles section, you will see a list of articles available in the library. You can read through them to enhance your knowledge.

2. Phrases Section:
In the Phrases section you will find a grid of phrases which offers bite-sized wisdom you can easily browse through.
