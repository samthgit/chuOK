Guide 1: Registering

There are two ways to register to chuOK:

1. Sign Up Button on Main Page:
Press the Sign Up button on the main page to access the Sign Up page.

2. Sign Up Button on Login Page:
Pressing the Sign Up link on the login page will get you to the Sign Up page.

Once you are on the Sign Up page, fill in the required fields:

1. Username: Choose a unique username that you will use to log in.

2. Email: Enter a valid email address that you have access to.

3. Password: Create a strong password that you will remember.

You are now part of the chuOK community!
